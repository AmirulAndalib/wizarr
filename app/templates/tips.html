How to Use Plex?

If you have accepted the email invitation from Plex, should be ready to start watching!

Good Question! Plex is a piece of software that helps people share media with each other. If you are here, someone wants to share their library with you! You'll be able to watch whatever they have on their server!


üçø Right, so how do I watch stuff?

It's easy! Plex is available on almost every device! Laptops, Tablets, Phones, TVs etc! All you have to do is download Plex on that device, sign in with your account and you're pretty much good to go!


üõã I've logged in but I am overwhelmed with content!

Yes, well, Plex is a free service, and so therefore, they have to make money somehow. The way they do so, is by pushing their own content before the content of the server you are joining. Don't be afraid however, this is easily fixable!

1. On your left, you should see a menu. Click on the Expand button. You will be presented will all the types of media you have access to.

2. We would recommend hiding all content put forward by Plex. To do this, select all the content types you don't want (such as 'Movies and TV On Plex') and 'Unpin' them from your home.

3. Now that everything we don't want is out of the way, we can focus on the things we do want!


üéû How can I make sure to have the best experience?

By default, Plex plays everything at a very low quality, which is a shame! Again, this is easily remedied.

1. Go to the Plex Settings page. This is usually in the top right hand corner, depending on the device you are on.

2. Go to the Quality or Video settings.

3. Set the Remote/Internet Streaming to Maximum/Original

4. Set the Local Streaming to Maximum/Original

There you go! If you experience any buffering, remember that at any time you can change the playback quality, just as you can in Youtube!
